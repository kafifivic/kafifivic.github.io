Hello and welcome to my website!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I am Victoria, a confident, determined and grounded individual. I live by the motto that by respecting others, I make a meaningful contribution to humanity.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I am an open minded and a very hard working person, characteristics I sharpen in each project I am involved with, both past and present. I do enjoy socialising with people from all walks of life, through many different activities such as taking long walks, traveling or just by engaging in any outdoors and indoors activities.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

As I work, every day, on becoming a better me, I aim in positively add towards making Humanity greater.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Despite being passionate about many other matters in life, my heart beats faster and dearly for Fashion and the charitable work I do in it. I have been privileged in working in the Fashion industry since 2008. In all these years I have experienced different areas from event coordinator, to casting director, to Runway&Fitness Coach and as Fashion model. I have been honoured to meet and work with many great people in this industry from whom I am inspired by, and expect to continue making my contribution using my Fashion expertise.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

For those of you who have been there since day one, many thanks for your caring and support. For those of you who are starting to know me and the new ones I hope you love this journey as much as I do! Let\u2019s make great memories together!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I hope that you enjoy your time here reading these pages!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Dearly Victoria<\/p>\n\n\n\n
